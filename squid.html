Squid Game

8/10 | Rate

Season 2 premieres Dec. 26 on Netflix

It’s been three years since the entire world cheered on Gi-hun (Lee Jung-jae) as he escaped a lethal game show and vowed to track down those responsible. Now, the unlikely hero returns to make good on his promise in Season 2, which sees Player 456 head back into the game with a whole new group of contestants ready to set their humanity aside for a chance at the grand prize. Prepare yourself for more tense conflicts, shocking reveals, and the return of Young-hee the murderous “Red Light, Green Light” doll. The new season drops on December 26, and we can’t think of a better way to close out the holidays than with this grim exploration of greed, capitalism, and human perseverance.
